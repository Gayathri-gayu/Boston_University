Boston College @ Glance.Education with a heart and soul—and the power to transform.

Boston College of Engineering and Technology was instituted in the year 1985. The college is accredited with “A” Grade by National Assessment and Accreditation Council, Bangalore.
The college has been granted autonomous status by UGC during 2011 and approved by AICTE, New Delhi and has a permanent affiliation to Anna University, Chennai. Housed in an area of 130 acres and with total built-up area of 80,000 sq.m., the college has excellent infrastructure-academic buildings, laboratories, library, hostels and play grounds where the students experience pleasant and congenial intellectual environment to grow with unique and value based academic experience.
